Hey there, I'm Erica.

I was born and raised in a D.C. suburb with an enormous curiosity for anything and everything. Aside from the people in my life, I cannot live without my planner, the September issue of Vogue and a smile. It’s how I live my life day to day but more importantly, what I can bring to you. It’s nice to meet you.

"The longer I live, the more beautiful life becomes."

- Frank Lloyd Wright

As a true optimist, I often see the beauty in everything. Even in the things that tend to be overlooked. In the behind the scenes. In the angle here, the kern there, the fleeting animation. Photography, Design, Development. My passions. The elements that push me to pursue a more beautiful life.

The endless journey.

It's been said that 'life is short but sweet for certain'. So why waste precious moments doing things that we don't love? I'm not sure if I'll ever know what my 'purpose' is or what I 'should be doing'. But I do know that every step I take in life (backed by a lot of love) guides me toward something greater than before. Did I know taking "Design For The Web I" (for fun) would change my career? Did I know I would find this oceanside gem when I decided to book a flight to California (three hours before it was set to depart)? Certainly not, but I guess that's why they say life is sweet for certain. At least it is on my endless journey.
